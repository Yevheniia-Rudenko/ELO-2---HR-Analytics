# Communication

## Communication Channels

### Primary Channels

- **GitHub Issues** - Task tracking and technical discussions
- **Pull Requests** - Code review and collaboration
- **Slack/Discord** - Quick questions and team coordination
- **Email** - Official communications and schedules

### Meeting Schedule

- **Weekly Sync** - Every Monday at 10:00 AM
- **Sprint Planning** - First Monday of each sprint
- **Retrospectives** - Last Friday of each sprint

## Communication Guidelines

1. **Be Respectful** - Treat all team members with respect
2. **Be Clear** - Write clear, concise messages
3. **Be Responsive** - Respond within 24 hours
4. **Be Constructive** - Provide actionable feedback
5. **Ask Questions** - No question is too small

## Response Times

- **Urgent** - Within 4 hours
- **Normal** - Within 24 hours
- **Low Priority** - Within 48 hours
